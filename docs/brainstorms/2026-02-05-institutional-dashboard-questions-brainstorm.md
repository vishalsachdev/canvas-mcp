# Aspirational Institutional Dashboard Questions

**Date:** 2026-02-05
**Status:** Brainstorm
**Author:** jdec + Claude

## Context

This document captures aspirational questions that Franklin School's leadership (deans, lead teachers, administration) might want to ask about their Canvas LMS. These questions inform future feature development for the Canvas MCP server.

**Franklin Context:**
- Grades 9-12 high school in Jersey City, NJ
- "World's Most Innovative Schools" (T4 Education 2025)
- Project-based, student-agency-focused curriculum
- 9 Transdisciplinary Competencies assessed across all subjects
- Skills Curriculum, Capstone Projects, Franklin Incubator, Micro-courses, Academic Care

---

## Questions by Stakeholder

### For the Head of School / Administration

**Strategic Overview**
- "What's the overall 'health score' of our school this semester compared to last year?"
- "Which grade level is thriving, and which needs attention?"
- "Are we living up to our 'learning by doing' philosophy? Show me the ratio of project-based vs. traditional assessments across all courses."
- "What percentage of student work demonstrates cross-disciplinary connections?"

**Competency Intelligence**
- "Which of our 9 TD Competencies are students excelling at school-wide?"
- "Which competencies have the largest gaps between Grade 9 and Grade 12?"
- "Are there achievement gaps in any competency by demographic group?"
- "Show me a heatmap of competency strength by subject area—where are the bright spots we can learn from?"

**Resource Allocation**
- "Which courses would benefit most from instructional design support based on engagement metrics?"
- "Where are teachers spending grading time that doesn't correlate with student learning gains?"
- "If we had to prioritize professional development, which competency should we focus on based on student outcomes?"

**Accreditation & Compliance**
- "Generate an accreditation-ready report mapping our courses to the 9 TD Competencies with evidence"
- "Are all courses consistently using the TD Competencies rubric?"
- "Show me FERPA compliance across all third-party tool integrations"

---

### For Deans / Division Heads

**Student Success Intelligence**
- "Which students across my division are showing early warning signs this week?"
- "Show me students who are excelling in one course but struggling in a related one—where are our scaffolding gaps?"
- "Which students have declined in engagement over the past 3 weeks?"
- "Predict which students might struggle with their upcoming Capstone based on current patterns"

**Faculty Effectiveness**
- "Which teachers have the highest student engagement, and what practices are they using?"
- "Compare discussion depth and quality across all sections of the same course"
- "Which courses have the widest gap between stated learning outcomes and actual assessment alignment?"
- "Show me teachers who haven't updated their course content in 2+ years"

**Cross-Departmental Patterns**
- "Find natural collaboration opportunities between my department and others based on student overlap and concurrent timing"
- "Which thematic connections in Humanities are actually showing up in student work across other subjects?"
- "Are our integrated science courses actually achieving integration, or are Bio/Chem/Physics still siloed?"

**Competency Tracking**
- "How are students in my division progressing across the 9 competencies this quarter?"
- "Which students are scoring D or F on Agency across multiple subjects?"
- "Compare Collaboration scores in Science labs vs. Design & Tech projects—are we measuring the same thing?"

---

### For Lead Teachers / Department Chairs

**Course Quality & Consistency**
- "Are all sections of my course providing equivalent learning experiences?"
- "Which of my assignments have the highest completion rate and quality?"
- "What discussion prompts in my department generated the most substantive student responses?"
- "Which rubric designs correlate with the fastest grading and clearest student feedback?"

**Student Support**
- "Which students in my classes need intervention this week based on engagement patterns?"
- "Show me students who are participating in discussions but struggling on assessments—they might need different support"
- "Which students haven't accessed course materials in the past week?"

**Curriculum Effectiveness**
- "Are my learning outcomes actually being assessed? Show me the alignment."
- "Which modules have the lowest completion rates, and why?"
- "Compare student performance on similar competencies across my courses—am I teaching it better somewhere?"

**Workload & Pacing**
- "What does a typical student week look like across my courses? Are we overwhelming them?"
- "Which assignments take the longest to grade but add the least measurable learning value?"
- "Are my due dates clustering with other courses' major assignments?"

---

### For Academic Care / Student Support

**Early Warning**
- "Which students across all courses are showing signs of disengagement?"
- "Show me students whose executive functioning scores in Academic Care don't match their academic performance—they might need different support"
- "Which students have missed multiple assignments across different courses this week?"
- "Identify students who are academically strong but showing social-emotional flags in reflections"

**Intervention Tracking**
- "Are our executive functioning interventions correlating with improved academic outcomes?"
- "Which Academic Care workshops have the highest impact on subsequent student performance?"
- "Show me students who improved after intervention—what worked?"

**Holistic View**
- "Give me a 360° view of this student: grades, engagement, competency scores, Academic Care notes, and peer review quality"
- "Which students are thriving with agency and choice, and which need more scaffolding?"

---

### For Skills Curriculum Coordinators

**Competency Development**
- "How are students progressing through the 9 competencies from Grade 9 to Grade 12?"
- "Which competency shows the most growth during Skills 100 vs. Skills 200?"
- "Are competency scores in Skills Curriculum correlating with competency scores in subject areas?"
- "Show me students who excel at Systems Thinking in Skills class but struggle with it in Science"

**Cross-Course Integration**
- "Which subject-area teachers are most effectively integrating TD Competencies into their grading?"
- "Are teachers across subjects calibrated on what 'A+ Collaboration' looks like?"
- "Find courses where the same competency is being taught concurrently—opportunity for cross-course projects"

**Rubric Analytics**
- "What's the grade distribution across each competency school-wide?"
- "Which competencies have the most variance in grading between teachers?"
- "Are any teachers consistently grading a competency higher or lower than the mean?"

---

### For Capstone Coordinators

**Project Health**
- "Which Grade 10 students are on track for their Mini Capstone (2,000 words)?"
- "Which Grade 11-12 students' Major Capstones are at risk based on current progress?"
- "Show me Capstone projects that haven't had mentor feedback in 2+ weeks"

**Quality Patterns**
- "What topics are trending in this year's Capstones vs. previous years?"
- "Which Capstone projects best exemplify multiple TD Competencies?"
- "Show me successful Capstones that could serve as exemplars for current students"

**Resource Matching**
- "Match students working on similar Capstone topics for peer collaboration"
- "Which external mentors or community partners align with this student's Capstone topic?"

---

### For Franklin Incubator Coordinators

**Pipeline Health**
- "What's the Incubator pipeline looking like—idea quality, mentor engagement, progress?"
- "Which Incubator projects are stalled and need intervention?"
- "Show me projects that have made the most progress this month"

**Competency Alignment**
- "Which TD Competencies are most exercised in Incubator projects?"
- "Are Incubator participants showing stronger Agency and Adaptability than non-participants?"

**Impact Tracking**
- "Have any Incubator projects led to internship placements or real-world implementation?"
- "What's the correlation between Incubator participation and overall academic engagement?"

---

### For Micro-Course Coordinators

**Engagement Patterns**
- "Which micro-courses have the highest enrollment and completion rates?"
- "Are micro-courses converting to deeper career interest (subsequent course selection, internships)?"
- "Which students are 'micro-course hoppers' vs. those who go deep in one area?"

**Career Pathway Insights**
- "Show me students interested in fintech—what's their academic profile across subjects?"
- "Are students in engineering micro-courses also excelling in Math and Design & Tech?"
- "Which career pathways are underexplored by our students?"

## The "Holy Grail" Questions

These would truly differentiate Franklin:

1. **"Why are students succeeding or failing?"** — Not just who, but causal analysis connecting engagement, competency scores, interventions, and outcomes

2. **"What should we teach differently next semester?"** — Prescriptive recommendations based on learning outcome gaps and student feedback patterns

3. **"Show me the student journey"** — Trace a student's path from Grade 9 to 12 across all competencies, courses, Capstones, and Incubator projects

4. **"Where is human time being wasted that AI could handle?"** — Identify grading, feedback, and administrative tasks that don't require human judgment

5. **"Are we actually transdisciplinary?"** — Evidence-based analysis of whether our courses are truly connecting, or just co-existing

6. **"What would the ideal Franklin graduate look like, and how far are our current students?"** — Gap analysis against the aspirational competency profile
